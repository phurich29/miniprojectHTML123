PRO-CENTER: Get to Know Yourself

At the starting point, everyone will come to building A3 to register and receive information about different faculties and majors. Seniors will introduce interesting highlights and the event schedule. Also, don’t miss the ‘FIND YOUR PRO-PASSION QUIZ’ to learn more about yourself, your passion, and which faculty suits you best. There will also be the PRO-STAGE for everyone to enjoy.

CLUSTER CENTER: Find Your Passion

Find your perfect major and activity at the Cluster Center, where people with the same DNA come together. Whether it's Business, Creative, Innovative, or BU International, this center will provide you with all the information and schedules for the events happening in each cluster.

PRO-ACTIVITIES: Real Experience

This area is for trying hands-on activities that help you become a pro. Get real experience with equipment and learn from professors and BU’s real-world partners. Meet and talk with seniors who will share their university life stories with you. It’s a place to explore and grow.

PRO-CERTIFICATE: Earn Certificates

One important highlight of this year is the ‘E-Certificate’ you’ll earn after participating in activities. Scan the QR code at each activity to receive your certificate and add it to your portfolio.

FAQ: Got Questions?

When and Where is the BU Open House 2024?
The event will take place on August 29-31, 2024 at Bangkok University Main Campus (Rangsit).
Do I need to register to attend?
You can pre-register for the BU Open House at this link or register on-site.
How many faculties can I join?
You can register once and choose to join multiple faculty activities!
What should I wear?
At Bangkok University, we accept diversity. You can wear either a university uniform or your own freestyle outfit as long as it is respectful and suitable for the academic environment.
When do the activities start and end?
Faculty activities will run from 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM, followed by special evening activities like concerts and performances at the central stage from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM.
Will there be faculty tours every day?
Yes, there will be activities every day for each faculty. Check the schedule on the main page for details as some highlights vary each day. Make sure you plan accordingly so you don’t miss out on what interests you most!
